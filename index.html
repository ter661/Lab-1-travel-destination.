Overview

Kyoto, the former imperial capital of Japan, is famous for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden machiya houses. It's a city steeped in history and renowned for its preserved cultural heritage.

Top Attractions

Don't miss these incredible sights!

  • Kiyomizu-dera Temple: A wooden stage offering panoramic views.
  • Fushimi Inari-taisha: Thousands of vibrant red torii gates.
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: A breathtaking, towering bamboo forest.
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): A stunning Zen Buddhist temple covered in gold leaf.
